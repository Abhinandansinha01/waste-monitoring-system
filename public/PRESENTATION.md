# Slide 2: The Problem (Why do we need this?)

**Current Challenges in Daily Life:**

1. **Improper Segregation:**
    * People often throw recyclables in general waste or hazardous batteries in regular bins.
    * *Result:* Contaminated recycling batches and environmental damage.
2. **Inefficient Collection:**
    * Garbage trucks visit empty bins (wasting fuel) or ignore full bins (causing overflow).
    * *Result:* High operational costs and public health hazards from overflowing trash.
3. **Lack of Data:**
    * Municipalities have no real-time data on which areas generate the most waste or what *kind* of waste it is.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution

**EcoSort AI: An Intelligent Ecosystem**
We propose a smart system that automates the thinking process of waste management.

1. **Automated Sorting:**
    * Uses **Computer Vision (AI)** to identify waste types automatically.
    * Removes human error from segregation.
2. **Smart Monitoring:**
    * IoT sensors track bin levels in real-time.
    * Alerts authorities *only* when collection is needed.
3. **Data-Driven Decisions:**
    * Provides analytics on waste generation patterns for better city planning.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features (Technical Overview)

1. **AI Waste Classifier (MobileNet/TensorFlow):**
    * Instantly identifies images as **Organic, Recyclable, or Hazardous**.
    * Runs efficiently in the browser/edge devices.
2. **Real-Time Bin Monitoring:**
    * Live dashboard showing fill-levels (0% to 100%).
    * Visual alerts (Red/Yellow/Green) for maintenance status.
3. **Analytics Dashboard:**
    * Tracks total waste sorted, contamination rates, and collection schedules.

---

## Slide 5: Future Scope (The Vision)

**Where can we take this next?**

1. **Smart City Integration:**
    * Connect with municipal garbage trucks for **Route Optimization** (Google Maps integration to pick up *only* full bins).
2. **Hardware Implementation:**
    * Integrate with a physical prototype using **Raspberry Pi** and **Servo Motors** to physically push waste into the correct bin.
3. **Gamification & Rewards:**
    * "Earn while you recycle": Users get points or crypto-tokens for correctly segregating waste.
4. **Advanced Plastic Detection:**
    * Upgrade AI to distinguish between different types of plastics (PET, HDPE) for higher recycling value.
